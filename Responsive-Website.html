Intro

▼
by: Malak Salama

Surah An-Nasr (Arabic: سورة النصر) is the 110th chapter of the Quran with 3 verses. It is one of the shortest surahs in the Quran. The surah is named "An-Nasr" which means "The Help" or "The Victory" in English.

This surah was revealed in Medina during the final years of the Prophet Muhammad's (PBUH) life. It speaks about the coming victory of Islam and the acceptance of the religion by the people in large numbers.

The main themes of this surah are:

  • Announcement of Allah's help and victory
  • People entering Islam in multitudes
  • Glorification and praise of Allah
  • Seeking forgiveness from Allah

Surah An-Nasr

▼
by: Malak Salama

Surah An-Nasr is a very important surah in the Quran. Here is the translation of its verses:

1. When the victory of Allah has come and the conquest,
2. And you see the people entering into the religion of Allah in multitudes,
3. Then exalt [Him] with praise of your Lord and ask forgiveness of Him. Indeed, He is ever Accepting of repentance.

This surah carries a profound message about gratitude to Allah for His blessings and the importance of seeking forgiveness even in times of victory and success.

Al-Aqsa University (Arabic: جامعة الأقصى) is a Palestinian university established in 1955 in the Gaza Strip, Palestine. Established in 1955 as the first higher education institution, Al-Aqsa University is the oldest governmental higher education institution. It caters for some 26000 students and has some 1400 employees, 300 of whom are lecturers and professors.
